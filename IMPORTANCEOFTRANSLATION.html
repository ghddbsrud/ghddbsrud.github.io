Why is translation important?

The skills of translation are becoming ever more important and desirable. 

Spread of Information and Ideas: Translation is necessary for the spread of information, knowledge and ideas. It is absolutely necessary for effective and empathetic communication between different cultures. Translation, therefore, is critical for social harmony and peace.

Global society: As communication and travel advance, geography is becoming less and less of a barrier to doing business. Companies benefit from working overseas. They can take advantage of the lower cost of products and services in some countries, the professional and industrial expertise of others, and additional markets to trade in.

Different language: Language is much more than the communication of words. It is also an expression of culture, society, and belief. Promoting a universal language, therefore, would likely lead to  a loss of the culture and heritage communicated through native languages.
